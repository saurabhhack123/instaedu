About DevGeek T-Shirts.

DevGeek T-shirts was an idea conceived after modest success on Kickstarter with a single t-shirt called The Super Geek Tee. After realizing there was an underserved
market for developer themed t-shirts, I came up with a handful of design concepts, elicited help from a good friend to film a video, and launched DevGeek T-shirts on
Kickstarter in April, 2013. About a month later, 469 backers made DevGeek T-shirts a success

Have a shirt idea?

Sometimes people tell us they have a great idea for a shirt. Is that you? Then open an issue on our shirt-ideas repo on GitHub and tell us about it!
